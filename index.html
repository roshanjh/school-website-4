Lucknow Public School, Sector 9, Vrindavan Yojna Lucknow, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day School, with classes from Pre-nursery to XII. It is an English, Hindi Medium school. The school is located in Vrindavan Yojna area of Lucknow. Lucknow Public School, Sector 9, Vrindavan Yojna was established in 2009. It is a Private School and is part of Lucknow Public Schools and Colleges and is managed by Lucknow Public Educational Society.

  • Online Attendence System

    Lucknow Public School with School Console (https://schoolconsole.net) launched India's first ever Android app for schools. This app is very helpful app for parents,students,teachers & management to get or upload information about student. Once the app is installed on the mobile phone, student,parent,teacher or management starts getting or uploading information for student or staff attendance, homework/assignment, results, event , calendar, fee dues, daily remarks, etc.
